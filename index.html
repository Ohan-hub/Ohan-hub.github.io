A Bit About Them

The Reptile Discovery Center houses over 70 different species of reptiles. They are funded by the government and by donations. They are located in Washington DC off Connecticut Avenue: 3001 Connecticut Ave. NW, Rock Creek Park, Washington, D.C., U.S.
